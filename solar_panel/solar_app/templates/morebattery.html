Battery Storage

Solar Harvest: Battery Banked Power

The converted AC electricity is directed to
a battery storage system. This is typically
composed of rechargeable batteries, such as
lithium-ion or lead-acid batteries, designed
to store the excess energy generated during
periods of high sunlight.

Powering Up with Battery Charge

As sunlight is abundant, the solar panels
generate electricity, which charges the
batteries during the day. The charge
controller ensures that the batteries
receive an optimal charge, preventing damage
and maximizing their lifespan.

Charging the Batteries

Discharge for Power Needs

Sun to Battery Power: Lighting Up the Night

When energy demand exceeds the current solar
production, or during nighttime when there
is no sunlight, the stored energy in the
batteries is discharged. The inverter converts
the stored DC energy back into AC, making
it suitable for powering appliances and devices.
