How Solar Products work?

Solar Panels

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity,
powering homes and businesses while reducing
fossil fuel dependence and promoting sustainability.

Solar Heater

Solar heaters use sunlight to warm water or
air. Panels collect sunlight, heating a
fluid inside. This hot fluid then heats
water or air for use, providing an
eco-friendly heating option..

Inveter

Inverters are essential in solar energy
systems as they convert the DC electricity
generated by solar panels into AC electricity,
making it usable for powering homes and
businesses.

Solar Cooker

Solar cookers use sunlight to cook food,
offering an eco-friendly and cost-effective
alternative to traditional
fuel sources.
